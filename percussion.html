Percussion

Percussion is a fancy word for saying “the drums”. In this story the specific drum is called the timpani. This instrument has anywhere from about 4 to 6 drums surrounding the player. Each drum makes a separate note that the player hits! The drums usually keep the beat or occasionally play the same beats as the brass sections to add more notes to it in order to make chords! There are many different instruments percussion uses like the slide whistle, xylophone, or the snare drum. Each instrument is used to make different sounds to add different feelings to the music.
