French Horn

The French Horn belongs to the brass family and was created originally in Germany. Bras means that it is made out of metal. The French Horn music usually plays the cool music like in Star Wars or Star Trek, but it also gets to play the fast moving back up music! The French Horn can play as low as a trombone and some Tubas and as high as a trumpet! Unlike the trumpet and trombone, the French Horn is a circle looking instrument that curls and plays the music away from the audience. Even though it points away from the audience, the French Horn is a very powerful and mighty instrument that can and will always be heard in the group!
